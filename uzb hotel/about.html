About Hotel Uzbekistan

The Uzbekistan Hotel is the legendary hotel, which has been receiving guests for more than thirty years, and can be considered as one of the symbols of the capital.

The multistorey building of the hotel, resembling a book by its shape, is located in the heart of the capital. There is the wonderful view of Amir Temur Square from windows of the hotel.
